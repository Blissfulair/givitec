About Us

WE ARE GIVITEC

GIVITEC LTD is an IT services and electronics installations contracts company.

The company also has a Vocational Training Institute, Real Estate practice and a JAMB Accredited CBT Centre. GIVITEC LTD is the business arm of GIMOX INSTITUTE FOR VOCATIONS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY aka GIVITEC INSTITUTE which was established on october 8, 2017 in Benin City.
